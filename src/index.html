Discover More

Take charge of your professional development.

Cardinal Academy is a leading center for emergency medicine education in Nigeria. Since 2010 we have worked with over 1500 students and trained them in the practice of modern, efficient emergency responsiveness. We offer 15 different courses including basic life support (BLS), acute cardiovascular life support (ACLS),
trauma management (ATLS), pediatric life support (PALS) and first aid. Our instructors are certified professionals and all our courses are internationally accredited by organizations like the American Heart Association and the Resuscitation Council of South Africa. Join our growing network of alumni utilizing their life saving skills around the world and discover what it means to have the cardinal advantage !

Globally Recognized

Cardinal academy issues certificates on behalf of globally recognized institutions like the American Heart Association (AHA) and the Resuscitation Council of South Africa (RCSA). Our alumni are thus able to work all over the world and in every industry imaginable including hospitals, industrial clinics, offshore locations, military formations, disaster relief zones, schools and universities and many others

Intensive

Our sessions are characterized by small class size. We combine theoretical and practical sessions in typically 4 day events lasting from 8am to 5pm with short relief breaks and lunch. There is much to learn and master and students usally come away from this experience exhausted but much more knowledgeable!

Flexible

Whether you are a doctor, nurse,paramedic or first-aider in need of certification or a large organization that needs to get its employees up to speed we have a course for you. For large groups, we conduct training for large candidate pools from teaching hospitals, oil and gas companies, universities, disaster relief agencies and other private organizations. Whatever your needs, we have you covered. Give us a call
