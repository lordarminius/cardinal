Cardinal Academy is the foremost institution for emergency medicine education in Nigeria.
Since 2010 we have worked with over 1500 students and trained them in the practice of modern, efficient, and rigorous emergency responsiveness.
We offer 15 different courses including basic life support (BLS), acute cardiovascular life support (ACLS), trauma management (ATLS), pediatric life support (PALS) first aid, and many others.
Our tutors are internationally certified professionals and all our courses are accredited by the American Heart Association and the Resuscitation Council of South Africa
